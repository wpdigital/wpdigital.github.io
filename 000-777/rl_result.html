Adventurous Investors do not typically regard themselves as cautious people and usually view risk as a source of opportunity rather than as a threat. They generally have substantial experience of investment and find investment matters easy to understand.


People in this category tend to make investment decisions quite quickly and are not generally anxious about the investment decisions they have made. They typically look for higher returns rather than safer investments and are comfortable investing in the Stockmarket. Adventurous investors prefer riskier, but higher returning, investments to keeping money in bank deposits.
